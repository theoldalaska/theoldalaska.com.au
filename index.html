Conceptualising

More than just a job shop, The Old Alaska is a resource that can work with you to bring a concept to life. If that means making parts from a napkin drawing or STEP file, to sitting down over a beer to work out a good solution to a problem.

A vast understanding of manufacturing and engineering technologies open up solutions that criss cross between disciplines, creating surprisingly cost effective and creative alternatives to traditional approaches.

Prototyping

A product can look great in drawings, but the amount that can be learned from holding a prototype cannot be quantified. Rapid prototyping is an important part of what we offer. With full in house CNC machining, CNC lathe turning and 3D printing, as well as traditional steel and aluminium fabrication capabilities - we are well equipped to create a prototype very early in the design process, ensuring an efficient design process.

Manufacturing

Similar to prototyping, we are well equipped to manufacture one-off parts and small production runs. But our real manufacturing capabilities are our proven relationships with our factories, both in Australia and China.

One special part that cant be sourced anywhere else, or productions runs of tens of thousands of parts, we dont mind - and are happy to work with anyone on any budget. We even offer packaging design, importation services and ecommerce website design!

Next Steps

Because we outsource as little as possible, and keep out overheads as close to 0 as we can, our prices are cheaper than most people think. We want everyone with an idea to be able to pursue it.

Get in touch and let us show you that with even a small investment, a part, prototype or project can realistically be realised.

We strive to be accessible, both in price and by maintaining a low barier to entry. We are more than happy to work off of a napkin drawing or a 15 minute chat on the phone. We find joy in helping people make things, and we are here to fill in the gaps to help you get an idea out of your head and into your hands.
